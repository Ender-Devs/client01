Our Story

TIKAWALA Prime Clean Solutions was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning, landscaping, and turf care services that transform spaces and enhance lives across Bokaro.

What started as a small team has grown into a trusted name in the region, serving hundreds of satisfied residential and commercial clients. We've built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and a genuine commitment to excellence.

Every project, whether it's a small home cleaning or a large commercial transformation, receives the same level of attention and care. We believe that quality work speaks for itself, and our clients' satisfaction is our greatest achievement.
